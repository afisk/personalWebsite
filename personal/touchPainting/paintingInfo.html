Touch Painting Project


Project Inspiration

A fun project to work on with Rashea Minor from chi.animalart.

We created a giraffe that has multiple areas that someone can touch and a sound will play.


Parts List

Adafruit Feather nRF52840 Express

Adafruit 12-Key Capacitive Touch Breakout MPR121

Adafruit Speaker - Plug & Play Audio Amplifier

Conductive Paint



Unmute

Turn on your volume to hear the sounds of the jungle.


Rashea painted the giraffe with both conductive and non-conductive black paint. There are 7 spots on the giraffe's neck that can play sounds when touched. The mane of the giraffe is used as a mode controller to switch out the sounds that play from the spots on the neck. We are using compressed .mp3 audio files as opposed to uncompressed .wav in order to pile more files into the limited space on our embedded system.

I was able to get the sleep current for this circuit down to 1.6 mA after configuring the nrf to sleep utnil the interrupt line from the touch breakout board is raised during a touch interaction. This circuit should be able to run around 18 weeks on a 6000 mAh battery with low usage.
