Bit Run Boards LLC was founded in July 2014, by Zach Powers. At 18 Zach wanted to start a business to help the growing communities of Makers, Hackers, and Startups in the Madison Wisconsin area. Since opening, Bit Run Boards has helped several startup business with launching their prototypes and in some cases, even helping launch the end-product. With interest from University of Wisconsin Faculty , pinball enthusiasts, electronic jewellery makers, and more, Bit Run Boards is committed to producing high quality product with short turnaround times and low volume minimums.
