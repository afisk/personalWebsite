"My Sweet Summer" - Dirty Heads (Borgeous Remix)
An upbeat tune just right for rolling your windows down to, My Sweet Summer delivers a smooth beat. Raving in the car has never been so fun!

"When We Were Young" - Dillion Francis (Valentino Khan Remix)
After this hit single dropped, Francis recruited various DJs to put together their best remixes. Khan speeds up the vocals to match his trademark sirenous productions in my favortie remix of Young.

"My Moment" - DJ Drama Feat. 2 Chainz, Meek Mill and Jeremih
I'm not sure how to explain this song 'cause it's just so good... The background for this track clinches its presence on this list. When combined with the softly autotuned vocals in the hook, DJ Drama mixes a winner. Now if only this song kept playing on the radio.

"Promises" - Nero (Skrillex Remix)
A rave and dubstep heavyweight, Promises is a killer track in itself. After a soft piano open, Skillex dumps the listener into is populer brostep swings, taking the track to new heights.
