Information about the eyes

Eyes are the part of the body that use light reflecting of objects in the world around us to send sensory signals to the brain which creates an image it believe is the world. Freaky stuff, huh? The Tarsier is an extremely cute and small primate that has massive eyes and is also mostly nocturnal. Tarsiers have much larger eye than humans in terms of body mass. Tarsiers are not naturally found in North America but are sometimes kept as pets (which is an awful idea, they are wild animals). More information can be found below!
