Motorcycles are interesting because:

  1. They get good gas mileage
  2. They are fun to ride
  3. There is nothing between you and the road, so you feel skillful if you can ride one

Information about motorcycles

Motorcycles are a form of transportation and entertainment. Overall, motorcycles have higher gas mileage ratings than a majority of cars. However, Motorcycles were not always around. In 1879 Giuseppe Munigotti patented the first gas powered motorcycle. The 1950s were known as the “golden age” for motorcycles. Some motorcycles only weighed around 150 pounds, had only 3.5 horsepower, and only cost a little over $200 back in 1911. Motorcycles do not need much horse power to be fast because of their weight.
